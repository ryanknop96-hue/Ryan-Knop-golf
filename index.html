The approach

How we work

01 Assess

We start with your current swing, your misses, and your goals. No rebuilds for the sake of it — we fix what's costing you shots.

02 Build

Clear, repeatable mechanics you can own. Ground-up fundamentals drawn from a real competitive background, not internet theory.

03 Pressure-test

A swing only counts if it holds up on the course. We train it until it survives the first tee and the back nine.

04 Own it

You leave each session knowing exactly what to practice and why. Progress you can feel, between lessons and into your scores.

Who you're learning from

The background

I played Division I college golf at Auburn University and was part of the 2018 SEC Championship team — a program that has since gone on to win two national championships.

I've also won tournaments across the country, including the Orlando International Amateur. That competitive background is what you're tapping into: real mechanics that hold up under pressure, not theory pulled off the internet. Whether you're chasing your first par or your next club championship, you'll get the same fundamentals that work when it counts.

On top of that, I've trained under some of the most respected teaching professionals in the country — taking lessons from Mark Blackburn, Jeff Smith, and Joseph Mayo — and I bring what I've learned from them straight into how I teach.
